\# Kitchen Domain Overview



\## Purpose



Defines the domain responsible for food preparation.



The Kitchen domain manages the operational workflow from receiving confirmed order items to completing food preparation.



The Kitchen domain does not own customer orders or payments.



\---



\## Responsibilities



The Kitchen domain is responsible for:



\* Receiving confirmed order items

\* Managing kitchen tickets

\* Managing kitchen queue

\* Tracking preparation progress

\* Publishing kitchen events



\---



\## Collaborates With



\* Ordering Domain

\* Table Domain



The Kitchen domain never modifies customer orders directly.
