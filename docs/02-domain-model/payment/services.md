\# Payment Domain Services



\## Purpose



Defines business operations within the Payment domain.



\---



\# Bill Calculation Service



Calculates the payable amount for a Bill.



\---



\# Payment Processing Service



Processes customer payments.



\---



\# Refund Service



Processes approved refunds.



\---



\# Bill Closing Service



Closes fully paid Bills.



\---



\## Principles



\* Services operate on Bill Aggregates.

\* Services never modify Ordering Aggregates directly.
