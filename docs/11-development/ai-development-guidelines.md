\# AI Development Guidelines



\## Purpose



Define how AI tools should assist development.



\---



\## AI May



Generate boilerplate.



Generate tests.



Suggest refactoring.



Generate documentation.



\---



\## AI Must Not



Change Business Rules.



Invent requirements.



Modify architecture without approval.



Change public APIs without review.



\---



\## Every AI change requires



Human review.



Architecture compliance.



Passing tests.
