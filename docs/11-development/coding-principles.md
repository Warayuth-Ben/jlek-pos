\# Coding Principles



\## Purpose



Describe the principles used when writing code.



\---



\## Principles



Readable over clever.



Simple over complex.



Explicit over implicit.



Composition over inheritance.



Business-first implementation.



\---



\## Design



Small functions.



Small classes.



High cohesion.



Low coupling.



\---



\## Maintainability



Prefer clarity.



Avoid duplication.



Remove dead code.



Refactor continuously.
