\# Takeaway Rules



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for takeaway orders.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Takeaway orders do not require a physical table.



The system may use a virtual service location for operational purposes.



\---



\### 2. Multiple takeaway orders may exist simultaneously.



Each order is managed independently.



\---



\### 3. Customers may pay before or after food preparation.



The payment sequence depends on business policy.



\---



\### 4. Completing one takeaway order does not affect other active orders.
