\# Walk-in Customer



\## Purpose



Define the business rules for walk-in customer orders.



\---



\## Description



A Walk-in Customer is a customer who places an order directly at the restaurant without using any online ordering channel.



Walk-in orders are processed immediately by the POS and may be assigned to either:



\- Dine-in Table

\- Takeaway



\---



\## Business Rules



\- Walk-in orders may be dine-in or takeaway.

\- Walk-in orders are created directly by staff.

\- Customer information is optional.

\- Payment may occur before or after food preparation depending on store policy.

\- Walk-in orders follow the standard Order Lifecycle.
