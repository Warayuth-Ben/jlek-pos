\# Delivery Rules



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for delivery orders.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Delivery orders originate from an external sales channel.



Examples include delivery platforms or direct business orders.



\---



\### 2. Delivery orders follow the same business lifecycle as other orders.



Channel-specific processing does not change the core lifecycle.



\---



\### 3. Customer delivery information may be attached to the order.



Delivery information is part of order fulfillment but does not alter the business identity of the order.



\---



\### 4. Order completion occurs when the restaurant finishes its operational responsibility.



Final customer delivery may be handled by an external delivery service.
