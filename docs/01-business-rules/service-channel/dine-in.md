\# Dine-in Rules



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for customers who consume food inside the restaurant.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Every dine-in order belongs to a table.



A dine-in order must always be associated with a valid table.



\---



\### 2. Multiple orders may exist for the same table.



Customers may place additional orders during their visit.



These orders belong to the same dining session.



\---



\### 3. Payment normally occurs after dining.



Customers typically pay when their dining session is complete.



\---



\### 4. Closing the bill releases the table.



After successful payment, the table becomes available for new customers.
