\# Service Channel Overview



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for different service channels supported by the POS system.



Each service channel represents a different customer journey while sharing the same business foundation.



\---



\## Supported Channels



The system currently supports:



\* Dine-in

\* Takeaway

\* Delivery



Additional service channels may be introduced in the future without affecting existing business rules.



\---



\## Common Principles



All service channels:



\* Create business orders.

\* Follow the standard order lifecycle.

\* Generate business history.

\* Support kitchen operations.

\* Support payment processing.

\* Produce business reports.



Each channel may define additional rules specific to its workflow.
