\# Business Context



\## Purpose



Defines the operational assumptions used throughout the POS system.



\---



\## Business Rules



The POS system is designed for restaurant operations.



The business operates through multiple service channels.



Examples include:



\* Dine-in

\* Takeaway

\* Delivery



Although each channel follows different workflows, they share the same business foundation.



Core business concepts remain consistent across all modules, including:



\* Orders

\* Items

\* Payments

\* Kitchen operations

\* Business history



All business rules documented in this project assume this shared operational context.
