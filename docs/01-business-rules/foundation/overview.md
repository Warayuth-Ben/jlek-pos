\# Foundation Overview



\## Purpose



This section defines the fundamental business rules that apply to the entire POS system.



These rules are independent of any specific feature or user interface and serve as the common foundation for all business processes.



Every module in the system must follow these rules unless explicitly stated otherwise.



\---



\## Scope



This section covers:



\* Order lifecycle

\* Bill numbering

\* Business events

\* Business context



These concepts are shared by every business module.
