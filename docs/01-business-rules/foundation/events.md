\# Business Events



\## Purpose



Defines significant business events that occur during system operation.



\---



\## Business Rules



Business events include, but are not limited to:



\* Order Created

\* Order Confirmed

\* Item Added

\* Item Removed

\* Item Updated

\* Kitchen Started

\* Kitchen Completed

\* Order Served

\* Payment Completed

\* Bill Closed



\---



Business events represent facts that have already occurred.



They may be used for:



\* Audit history

\* Reporting

\* Notifications

\* Integrations

\* Analytics



Business events are immutable once recorded.
