\# Business Numbering



\## Purpose



Defines the rules for generating business identifiers.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Every business document has a unique identifier.



Examples include:



\* Bill Number

\* Order Number

\* Kitchen Slip Number

\* Payment Number



\---



\### 2. Identifiers must never be reused.



Deleted or cancelled transactions do not free previously assigned numbers.



\---



\### 3. Business identifiers are permanent.



Once assigned, the identifier cannot change.



\---



\### 4. Identifier format is configurable.



The business may define prefixes, dates, counters, or other formatting rules.
