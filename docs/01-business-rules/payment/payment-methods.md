\# Payment Methods



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for accepting customer payments.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. The business may support multiple payment methods.



Examples include cash, card, bank transfer, and digital payment.



\---



\### 2. Every payment records the selected payment method.



The payment method becomes part of the business history.



\---



\### 3. Payment acceptance follows business policy.



Only approved payment methods may be used.



\---



\### 4. Successful payment authorizes bill completion.
