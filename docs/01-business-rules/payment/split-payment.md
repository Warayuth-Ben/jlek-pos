\# Split Payment



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for settling a bill using multiple payments.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. A bill may be paid using one or more payment methods.



Split payment is permitted when supported by business policy.



\---



\### 2. The combined payment amount must equal the final bill amount.



Underpayment and overpayment are handled according to business rules.



\---



\### 3. Each payment is recorded independently.



The complete payment history remains traceable.



\---



\### 4. A bill is considered fully paid only after all required payments have been successfully completed.
