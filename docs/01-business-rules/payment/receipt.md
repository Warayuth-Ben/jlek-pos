\# Receipt



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for issuing receipts after payment.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. A receipt may be generated after successful payment.



The receipt serves as proof of the completed transaction.



\---



\### 2. Receipt contents reflect the finalized business transaction.



Issued receipts are based on approved business data.



\---



\### 3. Reprinting a receipt does not create a new transaction.



The original business record remains unchanged.



\---



\### 4. Receipt history must remain traceable.
