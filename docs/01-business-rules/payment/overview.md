\# Payment Overview



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules governing payment processing and bill settlement.



Payment processing concludes the financial transaction between the customer and the restaurant while preserving complete business records.



\---



\## Scope



This section covers:



\* Bill calculation

\* Discounts

\* Payment methods

\* Split payment

\* Receipt generation

\* Refunds

\* Bill closure



These rules apply to every completed customer transaction.
