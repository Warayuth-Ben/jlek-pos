\# Refund



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for returning money after payment.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Refunds are separate business transactions.



A refund never deletes or replaces the original payment.



\---



\### 2. Refunds require business authorization.



Only approved refunds may be processed.



\---



\### 3. Refund amounts are recorded independently.



The original payment history remains unchanged.



\---



\### 4. Every refund becomes part of the permanent audit trail.
