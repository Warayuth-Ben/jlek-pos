\# Bill Closure



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for completing and closing a customer bill.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. A bill may be closed only after all payment requirements have been satisfied.



An unpaid bill cannot be closed.



\---



\### 2. Bill closure marks the end of the business transaction.



No further operational processing occurs on the bill.



\---



\### 3. A closed bill becomes read-only.



Business information is preserved without modification.



\---



\### 4. Bill closure is permanently recorded.



Closed bills remain available for reporting, auditing, and historical reference.
