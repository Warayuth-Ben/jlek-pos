\# Discounts



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for applying discounts.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Discounts are optional business adjustments.



A bill may contain none, one, or multiple discounts according to business policy.



\---



\### 2. Every discount must have a valid business reason.



The applied discount becomes part of the business record.



\---



\### 3. Discounts are included in the final bill calculation.



The payable amount reflects all approved adjustments.



\---



\### 4. Applied discounts remain part of the permanent business history.
