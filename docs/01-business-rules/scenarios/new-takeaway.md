\# New Takeaway Order



\## Purpose



Describes the standard business process for creating a new takeaway order.



\---



\## Actors



\* Customer

\* Restaurant Staff

\* POS System

\* Kitchen Staff



\---



\## Preconditions



\* The restaurant is operating.

\* The menu is available.



\---



\## Trigger



A customer places a takeaway order.



\---



\## Main Flow



1\. Staff creates a new takeaway order.

2\. Menu items are added.

3\. Optional item notes and options are entered.

4\. The order is reviewed.

5\. The order is confirmed.

6\. The kitchen receives the confirmed items.

7\. Food is prepared.

8\. The order is packaged.

9\. Payment is completed according to business policy.

10\. The customer receives the order.

11\. The bill is closed.



\---



\## Alternative Flows



\### Customer adds more items



Additional items are processed as an Add-on Order.



\---



\### Customer cancels the order



Cancellation follows the standard business rules.



\---



\## Postconditions



\* The takeaway order is completed.

\* Payment has been recorded.

\* Business history has been updated.
