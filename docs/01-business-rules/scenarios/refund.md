\# Refund



\## Purpose



Describes the business process for refunding a completed payment.



\---



\## Actors



\* Customer

\* Restaurant Staff

\* POS System

\* Manager (if required)



\---



\## Preconditions



\* A completed payment exists.

\* Refund is permitted by business policy.



\---



\## Trigger



A refund is approved.



\---



\## Main Flow



1\. Staff locates the completed transaction.

2\. Refund details are reviewed.

3\. Authorization is obtained if required.

4\. The refund is processed.

5\. Refund records are created.

6\. Business history is updated.



\---



\## Alternative Flows



\### Partial refund



Only the approved amount is refunded.



\---



\### Refund request rejected



The original payment remains unchanged.



\---



\## Postconditions



\* Refund information has been recorded.

\* Original payment history is preserved.

\* Audit records remain complete.
