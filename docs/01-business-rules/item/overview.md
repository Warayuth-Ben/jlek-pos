\# Item Overview



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules governing items within a customer order.



An order item represents a menu product requested by the customer and contains all business information required for preparation, pricing, and billing.



\---



\## Scope



This section covers:



\* Item quantity

\* Item options

\* Item notes

\* Item pricing

\* Item availability



These rules apply to every order item regardless of service channel.
