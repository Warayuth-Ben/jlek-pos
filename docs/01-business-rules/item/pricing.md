\# Item Pricing



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for determining the price of an order item.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Every order item has a business price.



The price is determined according to the business pricing policy.



\---



\### 2. The recorded price is fixed once accepted.



Future menu price changes do not affect existing orders.



\---



\### 3. Item options may influence pricing.



Additional charges or adjustments follow business configuration.



\---



\### 4. The total item value is calculated using the approved quantity and recorded price.
