\# Item Notes



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for customer instructions attached to an order item.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. An order item may include one or more preparation notes.



Notes provide additional instructions for restaurant staff.



\---



\### 2. Notes do not change the identity of the menu item.



They modify preparation only.



\---



\### 3. Kitchen staff should receive item notes during food preparation.



Notes are considered operational instructions.



\---



\### 4. Item notes become part of the business record.



They remain available for history and auditing.
