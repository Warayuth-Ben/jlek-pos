\# Item Availability



\## Purpose



Defines business rules governing whether a menu item may be ordered.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Only available menu items may be ordered.



Unavailable items cannot be added to new orders.



\---



\### 2. Availability is determined by business policy.



Availability may depend on inventory, operating hours, or manual business decisions.



\---



\### 3. Existing orders remain valid after availability changes.



Making an item unavailable affects future orders only.



\---



\### 4. Availability changes become effective immediately unless otherwise defined by business policy.
