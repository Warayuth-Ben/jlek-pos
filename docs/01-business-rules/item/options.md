\# Item Options



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for configurable menu options.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Menu items may support configurable options.



Examples include size, meat selection, drink choice, or other business-defined variations.



\---



\### 2. Options become part of the order item.



They define the exact customer request.



\---



\### 3. Option availability depends on the selected menu item.



Only supported options may be chosen.



\---



\### 4. Option selections are included in kitchen preparation and billing when applicable.
