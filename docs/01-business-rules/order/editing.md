\# Order Editing



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for modifying an existing order.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Orders may be edited only while business policy allows.



The editable period depends on the current stage of processing.



\---



\### 2. Editing may include adding, removing, or updating order items.



Each modification must preserve business consistency.



\---



\### 3. Every modification becomes part of the business history.



Previous business activity must remain traceable.



\---



\### 4. Completed orders cannot be edited.



Administrative corrections require separate business transactions.
