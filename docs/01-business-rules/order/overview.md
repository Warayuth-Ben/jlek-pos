\# Order Overview



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules governing the creation, modification, confirmation, fulfillment, and completion of customer orders.



An order represents a customer's request for one or more menu items and serves as the central business object throughout restaurant operations.



\---



\## Scope



This section covers:



\* Order creation

\* Order editing

\* Order confirmation

\* Add-on orders

\* Order cancellation

\* Order completion



These rules apply to all supported service channels unless explicitly stated otherwise.
