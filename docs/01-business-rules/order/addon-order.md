\# Add-on Orders



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for additional customer requests after an order has already been created.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Customers may place additional orders during the same business session.



Additional requests extend the existing customer transaction.



\---



\### 2. Each add-on order is recorded independently.



Business history distinguishes the original order from subsequent additions.



\---



\### 3. Kitchen processing applies only to newly added items.



Previously completed kitchen work is unaffected.



\---



\### 4. The final bill includes all approved add-on orders.



Billing reflects the complete customer transaction.
