\# Order Confirmation



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for confirming customer orders.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Confirmation indicates that the order has been accepted for processing.



The order becomes an operational commitment.



\---



\### 2. Only confirmed orders may proceed to kitchen preparation.



Unconfirmed orders remain pending.



\---



\### 3. Confirmation records the official start of business fulfillment.



Subsequent business processes depend on this event.



\---



\### 4. Confirmation becomes part of the permanent business history.



The confirmation event cannot be removed.
