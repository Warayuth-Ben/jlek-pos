\# Order Completion



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for completing customer orders.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. An order is completed only after all required business activities have finished.



Completion represents the end of operational processing.



\---



\### 2. Completed orders become read-only.



Business information is preserved without further modification.



\---



\### 3. Completion records the final operational state of the order.



Subsequent reporting uses this state for business analysis.



\---



\### 4. Order completion becomes part of the permanent business history.



Completed orders remain available for future reference, auditing, and reporting.
