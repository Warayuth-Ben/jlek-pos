\# Order Cancellation



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for cancelling customer orders.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Orders may be cancelled only when permitted by business policy.



Cancellation depends on the current operational stage.



\---



\### 2. Cancellation never removes historical business records.



Cancelled orders remain available for auditing and reporting.



\---



\### 3. Cancelled items are excluded from future business processing.



They are no longer prepared, served, or billed.



\---



\### 4. Every cancellation must record the business event.



The cancellation history forms part of the permanent audit trail.
