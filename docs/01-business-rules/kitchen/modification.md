\# Kitchen Modification



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for handling order changes after kitchen processing has begun.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Order modifications may occur after kitchen work has started.



Business policy determines which changes are permitted.



\---



\### 2. New items are processed as additional kitchen work.



Previously completed preparation remains unaffected whenever possible.



\---



\### 3. Cancelled items follow business cancellation procedures.



Kitchen staff must be informed of affected items.



\---



\### 4. Every kitchen modification is recorded.



The complete operational history remains traceable.
