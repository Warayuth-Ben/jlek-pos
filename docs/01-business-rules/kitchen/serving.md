\# Food Serving



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for delivering prepared food to the customer.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Food may be served only after preparation is complete.



Incomplete items cannot be served.



\---



\### 2. Every served item is considered fulfilled by the kitchen.



Responsibility transfers from kitchen preparation to customer service.



\---



\### 3. Serving status becomes part of the business history.



The serving event is permanently recorded.



\---



\### 4. Serving completes the kitchen responsibility for the item.



Subsequent business processes continue outside kitchen operations.
