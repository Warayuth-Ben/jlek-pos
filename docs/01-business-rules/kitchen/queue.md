\# Kitchen Queue



\## Purpose



Defines how confirmed order items enter and progress through the kitchen workflow.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Only confirmed order items enter the kitchen queue.



Unconfirmed items are not prepared.



\---



\### 2. Each queued item represents work that the kitchen must perform.



The queue reflects pending kitchen activities.



\---



\### 3. Queue order follows business policy.



Priority may depend on confirmation time, service channel, or operational rules.



\---



\### 4. Kitchen queue status reflects current preparation progress.



Queue information must accurately represent operational reality.
