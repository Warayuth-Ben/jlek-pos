\# Food Preparation



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for preparing customer orders.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Preparation begins only after the order has been confirmed.



Confirmation authorizes kitchen operations.



\---



\### 2. Kitchen staff prepare food according to the approved order.



Preparation includes item options and customer notes.



\---



\### 3. Preparation status must be recorded.



Business history reflects the progress of kitchen operations.



\---



\### 4. Completed preparation makes the order ready for serving.
