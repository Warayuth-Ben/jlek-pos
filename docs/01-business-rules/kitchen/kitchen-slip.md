\# Kitchen Slip



\## Purpose



Defines the business rules for documents sent to the kitchen.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Kitchen slips communicate food preparation requirements.



They contain only the information necessary for kitchen operations.



\---



\### 2. A kitchen slip reflects the approved business state at the time it is generated.



Subsequent order changes require additional business processing.



\---



\### 3. Kitchen slips may be generated multiple times.



Each slip represents a separate business event.



\---



\### 4. Kitchen slip history must remain traceable.



Reprints and additional slips do not replace previous business records.
