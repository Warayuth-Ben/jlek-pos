\# Table Transfer



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for moving an active dining session from one table to another.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. A dining session may be transferred to another table.



The transfer changes only the physical seating location.



\---



\### 2. Order identity remains unchanged after transfer.



Bill numbers, order history, and business events are preserved.



\---



\### 3. The original table becomes available only after the transfer is completed.



The destination table assumes responsibility for the active dining session.



\---



\### 4. Every table transfer must be recorded.



Transfer history forms part of the business audit trail.
