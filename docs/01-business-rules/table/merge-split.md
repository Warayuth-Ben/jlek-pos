\# Table Merge and Split



\## Purpose



Defines business rules for combining and separating table sessions.



\---



\## Business Rules



\### 1. Multiple dining tables may be merged into a single business session.



Merged tables operate as one dining group.



\---



\### 2. A merged session may later be separated if permitted by business policy.



The business history must remain consistent after separation.



\---



\### 3. Splitting a table must preserve order ownership.



Each resulting dining session must clearly identify its associated orders.



\---



\### 4. Merge and split operations must never lose business information.



All original business records remain traceable.
